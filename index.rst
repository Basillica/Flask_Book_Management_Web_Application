Welcome to BookApp's documentation!
===================================
A flask Application for easy book management. Users can 
sign in or sign up if they are new users. They can equally
create new books, edit them or delete them.

There is also an available Json-API that could be easily reached
like 'site.address.com/books'.

Requirements:
Python 3.7
Flask
